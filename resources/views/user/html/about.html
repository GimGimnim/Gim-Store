An Introduction to GIM Autos

Welcome to GIM Autos! We've created this page to introduce ourselves to you and to let you know what it is we do around here. GIM Autos is an automotive sales and services company covering the newest car offerings, showcasing car culture, and helping people shopping for a car by serving up our unique brand of intelligence, independence, and irreverence. We've been in the automotive business since 1965 and online for more than two decades.

We make the complicated easier to understand for those of you shopping for a new car and push the limits of performance machines for our enthusiast clients. A GIM Autos review balances the subjective with objective data and pulls no punches. We pull together multi-car comparison tests that separate the good, the bad, and the ugly to give you the product knowledge that you crave. Our online ranking system breaks down vehicle segments to help shoppers make informed decisions.

Our Workshop

GIM Autos is a specialist in automotive repair and maintenance services. The company offers best-in-class services using fully computerized processes and genuine spare parts. Our team has been trained to cater to the specific needs of each of the vehicle brands we specialize in. Our workshops are equipped with the most recent and sophisticated diagnostic equipment for each vehicle brand, with over 50 work Bays, a Body Section, Alignment and Balancing Sections, as well as highly-trained and experienced mechanical staff.
